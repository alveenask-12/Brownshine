Our Philosophy

In our atelier in Grasse, every LUMIÈRE fragrance begins as an emotion — a memory of rain, a field at dusk, a lover's warmth. Our master perfumer translates feeling into scent, blending rare materials gathered from across the world.

Sourcing
All raw materials are ethically sourced from their regions of origin — rose from Bulgaria, oud from Southeast Asia, sandalwood from Mysore.
Craftsmanship
Each batch is hand-blended and aged for a minimum of 8 weeks before bottling, ensuring depth and longevity.
Packaging
Every flacon is mouth-blown by artisan glassmakers in Lyon, sealed with a hand-lacquered cap.

Signature Ingredients

Rose Absolue

Harvested at dawn in the valleys of Grasse — rich, honeyed and warm with heart.

  • Origin: Grasse, France
  • Note: Heart
  • Character: Warm, honeyed, feminine
Aged Oud

Sacred resin from agarwood trees centuries old — smoky, rich and transcendent.

  • Origin: Cambodia
  • Note: Base
  • Character: Deep, smoky, meditative
Ambergris

The rarest oceanic treasure — amplifies every note and lends radiant longevity.

  • Origin: Atlantic Ocean
  • Note: Fixative
  • Character: Marine, warm, luminous
Mysore Sandalwood

Creamy, milky, and endlessly smooth — the timeless Indian base that holds all else.

  • Origin: Mysore, India
  • Note: Base
  • Character: Silky, warm, persistent
